Verse 1

Through storms and silence, we hold on tight,
Distance may linger, but we'll make it right.
Some nights feel like walking on glass,
Afraid one crack could make us collapse.
But even when shadows stretch too long,
Your voice reminds me where I belong.

Verse 2

Moonlight shines where my hands can't go,
But somehow, you’re always here, I know.
Pages turn, our story grows,
Laughter in movies only we know.
You showed me love is safe and real,
A place to breathe, a place to heal.

Verse 3

One day, the miles won’t keep us apart,
No more waiting, no more aching heart.
I’ll wake beside you, hold you close,
Feel your warmth, the touch I know.
No more distance, no more in-between,
Just you, my baby, right here with me.

Verse 4

On this day, I just want you to see,
You’re my forever, my love, my plea.
No chocolates or roses could ever compare,
To the love we’ve built, the life we’ll share.
So take my words and keep them tight—
Happy Valentine’s, my love, my light.
